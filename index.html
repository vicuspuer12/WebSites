Who We Are

The company was created and incorporated in 2012 as a private company by Mr. Valantine Takwa who is a qualified Mechanical Engineer with a Masters Degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Johannesburg.

  • Our Vision

    Our vision is to provide our clients with a one stop service and also to provide informative, appropriate and cost effective service based on our marketing principles and engineering solutions.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is to provide an attractive range of technical services and Engineering solutions that encourage potential clients to do business with us. We will achieve this by fair pricing, optimum use of staff and technology, multiple cross-linking for maximum service delivery, driven by our enthusiasm, creativeness and service commitment. We put our customers first and their satisfaction is our main concern.

  • Our Values

    Our key and unique value is to EMPOWER WOMEN in the engineering sector especially automotive service and engineering. Thus we target a 70% female workforce.

Our core business is the provision of Mechanical Engineering, Renewable Energy Consulting, New product designs, System installation and maintenance, Fire protection Engineering, Solar PV installation and Self-cleaning Techniques. We provide maintenance to buildings, industrial facilities, schools and government institution.
